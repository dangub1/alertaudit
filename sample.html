Methodology — identical in every report
  1. Source material. Every public post in the window collected and counted. Nothing excluded.
  2. Scoring rule. A post is a scoreable advance signal if it names an instrument, a direction, an entry level, and was posted before the move it describes. Exits follow the signal's own targets/stop.
  3. Price data. 1-hour exchange OHLC from the post's timestamp — no look-ahead. Entry rule: first 1h open after the post if inside the stated zone, else first touch of the zone edge.
  4. Conservative tie-break. A candle touching both target and stop counts the stop first.
  5. The $10K test. Equal split across all scoreable signals, no leverage. Benchmark: BTC buy-and-hold, same window.
  6. Uncertainty. Hit rates carry Wilson 95% confidence intervals. Small samples are stated, not hidden.
  7. Independence. No relationship with any signal provider. Educational statistical analysis, not financial advice.
